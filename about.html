About Us

Who We Are and Why We Started This

The Honest Version

MoneyWise started because we got tired of the same recycled money advice. You know the type — "invest in a diversified portfolio," "cut your morning coffee," "start a dropshipping business in 6 easy steps." It all sounds fine until you actually try to do it and realize half of it doesn't work the way it's described.

We wanted something different. Practical. Real. No affiliate product spam. No fake "I made $50,000 in one month" stories. Just honest breakdowns of what actually works for normal people who are trying to make a little more money.

What We Write About

Mostly three things:

Side hustles and making money — flipping stuff, local service businesses, freelancing, and anything else with a realistic shot at putting extra money in your pocket without requiring a massive investment up front.

Investing basics — for when you've got money coming in and want to start making it work for you. We keep it simple because most people don't need complicated.

Saving and budgeting — because making more money only helps if you're not burning through it just as fast.

We're not financial advisors. Nothing on this site is financial advice. It's just stuff we've researched, tested, and found useful. Make your own decisions — you know your situation better than we do.

What We're Not

We're not here to sell you a course. We're not going to tell you that you can make $10,000 a month working 2 hours a day. We're not going to recommend some sketchy app because someone paid us to.

If something doesn't work or has real downsides, we'll tell you that too. The goal is just to be actually useful — which means being honest even when the truth is boring or inconvenient.

Get In Touch

Have a question, a topic suggestion, or just want to share something that worked for you? We'd genuinely love to hear it. Reach out anytime.

Thanks for reading MoneyWise. Now go make some money.
