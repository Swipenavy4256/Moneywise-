15 Things You Can Flip From Around Your House for Fast Cash

July 20, 2026 · 7 min read · by MoneyWise
Before you buy anything to resell, look around — most people are sitting on hidden cash without realizing it. Here are 15 categories that consistently sell fast on Facebook Marketplace, eBay, or Poshmark.

Start With What You Already Own

You don't need startup capital to flip your first item — you need an inventory audit of your own closets, drawers, and garage. Here are the categories that move fastest.

15 Things That Sell Fast

  1. Old phones and tablets — even cracked or outdated ones have resale or parts value.
  2. Unused gift cards — sell partial-balance cards on gift card exchange sites for 85–95 cents on the dollar.
  3. Name-brand shoes — especially sneakers in good condition; check resale value before pricing.
  4. Textbooks — college textbooks especially, sold directly to students or via buyback sites.
  5. Video games and consoles — bundle old titles together for a faster sale.
  6. Kids' clothes and toys in good condition — parents actively search for gently used bundles.
  7. Tools you only used once — power tools resell surprisingly well.
  8. Furniture you're replacing — post it before donating; furniture moves fast locally.
  9. Kitchen appliances — stand mixers, air fryers, blenders you don't use.
  10. Unopened beauty and skincare products — a growing resale category on dedicated apps.
  11. Sports and exercise equipment — especially anything bought during a "new year, new me" phase.
  12. Books in good condition — sell in bulk lots for faster turnaround than one at a time.
  13. Collectibles and vintage items — check completed listings before pricing to avoid underselling.
  14. Spare electronics cables and accessories — bundle and sell as a lot.
  15. Designer or barely-worn handbags — consignment apps handle authentication and payment for you.

💰 Quick Pricing Tip

Before listing anything, search "sold listings" (not just active ones) on the platform you're using. That shows what buyers actually paid — not just what sellers are hoping for.

Where to Sell What

Local pickup items (furniture, appliances) do best on Facebook Marketplace. Shippable items (electronics, collectibles, clothing) often get better prices on eBay or category-specific apps like Poshmark.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I realistically make flipping items from my house?

Most people find $150–400 worth of sellable items on a first pass through closets, garages, and drawers. It varies widely depending on what you own.

What sells the fastest?

Electronics, name-brand shoes, and kids' items tend to move within days. Collectibles and niche items can take longer but often sell for more.
